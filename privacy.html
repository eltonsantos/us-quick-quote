Privacy Policy — US Quick Quote

Last updated: December 25, 2025

Summary: The US Quick Quote extension does not collect, sell, or share personal data. It only fetches public market quotes when you request them.

Data Collection

No personal data is collected.

Data Stored on Your Device

  • Optionally, the extension may store locally the last searched ticker for convenience (via chrome.storage).

This data stays only in your browser and is not sent to any developer server.

Market Data Sources

When you search for a ticker or load the "top gainers/losers", the extension makes HTTPS requests to Stooq endpoints (e.g., stooq.com) to obtain public market information.

Data Sharing

The extension does not share data with third parties. Requests to Stooq contain only the queried symbols (tickers) needed to return quotes.

Permissions

  • storage: to save locally the last searched ticker (optional).
  • host permissions (Stooq): to allow fetching quotes.

Advertising, Analytics, and Tracking

The extension does not display ads, does not use analytics, and does not track browsing.

Contact

If you need support or wish to remove local data, simply uninstall the extension (this also removes local storage).
